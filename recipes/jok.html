Jok Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for jok or congee. This is a delicious recipe for a cold day that requires minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of uncooked rice
  • 1 lb of chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/2 lb of ham
  • 1 tbsp of green onion

Steps

  1. Put the uncooked rice and chicken breast into a large pot.
  2. Add 9 cups of water to the pot and set to boil
  3. Once the pot is boiling, turn down the temperature to a simmer
  4. Let the pot simmer for 2 hours.
  5. After 2 hours, shred the chicken breast inside the pot with 2 forks.
  6. Dice the green onion.
  7. Cut the ham into small bite size pieces.
  8. Mix the soy sauce, ham and green onion into the pot.
  9. Enjoy!
