Peanut Butter Smooth Recipe

This is a peanut butter banana smooth recipe that is perfect for a postwork out meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 spoonful of peanut butter
  • 3 ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of protein powder

Steps

  1. In a blender, add all ingredients
  2. Blend until there are no ice chunks left
  3. Enjoy!
